Our story

Australian Linen & Towels was founded with a clear purpose: to make commercial-grade hospitality linen accessible to operators who care about quality but don't want to deal with bloated minimums, slow imports, or rigid wholesale terms.

We're based in Griffith, in the heart of the Riverina region of NSW — a part of Australia best known for its wineries, food culture, and thriving tourism economy. From here, we serve hotels, motels, day spas, gyms, beauty salons, and serviced accommodation across every state and territory.

The hospitality industry runs on small details. A guest's first impression of a room is rarely the artwork or the lighting — it's the sheets they touch when they sit on the bed, and the towels they reach for after a shower. That's our entire focus.

Who we supply

Our customers are operators across the Australian hospitality industry:

  • Hotels and motels — from boutique 12-room properties to 200-key business hotels
  • Day spas and beauty salons — where towel softness and colour-fastness matter daily
  • Gyms and fitness centres — high-turnover, durable hand and bath towels
  • Serviced apartments and Airbnb operators — consistent, hotel-grade bedding
  • Resorts and wellness retreats — premium linen for guest-facing spaces
  • Aged care and healthcare facilities — durable bed linen for institutional use

Whether you're managing a single-property motel in regional NSW or coordinating linen across a chain, we'll work with you the same way: directly, transparently, and with no pressure to over-order.

Why Griffith?

The Riverina is a working region. It has real businesses, real seasons, and a tourism economy built on motels, wineries, and family-run accommodation. Operating from here, rather than a distant capital city, keeps us close to the kind of customers we serve — and grounded in the practical realities of running hospitality in Australia.

From Griffith, freight reaches every major Australian city. We dispatch quickly, ship Australia-wide, and offer free freight on orders over AU$100.
