Spa & Salon Buyer FAQ

Common questions from spa and salon owners.

What towels do salons and spas use?

Salons typically use 350–450 GSM towels (often black to resist tint and bleach stains) for hair services. Day spas prefer 500–600 GSM for treatment towels and 600+ GSM for face washers. We supply all variants — see our GSM guide for details.

Do you stock black salon towels?

Yes — black salon towels are one of our highest-volume products. Black is preferred in hair salons because it disguises hair dye and bleach stains, extending the usable life of the towel significantly. Available in multiple sizes.

Do you supply bathrobes for spas?

Yes. We stock 100% cotton terry bathrobes suitable for guest amenities, treatment rooms, and post-treatment wear. Available in multiple sizes including unisex small/medium/large.

How do I stop my salon towels from fading?

Use vat-dyed (not surface-dyed) towels — the colour goes through the entire fibre, so hot commercial washing doesn't fade them. All our black and charcoal salon towels are vat-dyed.

Can I order branded coloured towels?

Yes — our coloured range (aqua, mint, cherry, fuchsia, mirage, etc.) suits salon and spa branding. Custom embroidery available through our supplier network on request.
