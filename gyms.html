Gym Buyer FAQ

Common questions from fitness centre operators.

What GSM is best for gym towels?

Gym and fitness centre towels work best at 350–450 GSM — light enough to dry quickly between member uses and economical to launder at high turnover, while still feeling substantial. See our GSM guide.

What towels do pools use?

Pool towels typically run 300–400 GSM — lighter for fast drying between member uses. Pool stripe patterns (cabana-style) are popular for resort pools and member-facing aesthetics.

Can I get embroidered gym towels?

Yes — custom embroidery is available through our supplier network for branded gym and studio towels. Minimum order quantities and lead times vary, contact us for a quote.

Do you do hire-program towel restocks?

Yes. Many of our gym clients run member towel hire programs and re-stock in regular batches. We can hold consistent stock and dispatch quickly for refresh orders.

What's the difference between gym towels and pool towels?

Gym towels are typically 350–450 GSM and plain white, charcoal, or navy. Pool towels are 300–400 GSM and often striped (cabana) for visual appeal at pool decks. Both prioritise fast drying over plushness.
