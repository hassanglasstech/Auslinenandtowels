Airbnb Host FAQ

Common questions from short-stay hosts.

What linen does an Airbnb host need?

A complete Airbnb starter kit includes 2 sets of bed sheets per bed (1 in use, 1 spare), at least 2 bath towels per guest, hand towels, face washers, a mattress protector, quilt and quilt cover, pillows with protectors. We can recommend exact quantities based on your guest capacity — get a tailored kit quote.

Do I need hotel-grade linen for Airbnb?

Yes if you want strong reviews. Reviews repeatedly mention towel and sheet quality — soft, plush, and clean. 500 GSM bath towels and 200TC percale sheets are the practical sweet spot for short-stay properties. See our GSM guide for details.

Can I order without an ABN?

Individual Airbnb hosts can purchase at our retail-equivalent quote pricing. For trade pricing, you'll need an ABN — but many small hosts choose to register an ABN once they're hosting regularly. Talk to us either way.

How often should I replace Airbnb linen?

For a busy short-stay property (washed after each guest), expect 7–8 months from a commercial 500 GSM towel before it shows wear. Replace sheets when they start pilling or losing crispness — see our replacement frequency guide.

What colour towels are best for Airbnb?

White is the safest choice — guests associate it with cleanliness and bleach-cleaning gives consistent presentation. Charcoal hides occasional staining for forgiving turnover. Camel is a warm alternative that hides light marks.
