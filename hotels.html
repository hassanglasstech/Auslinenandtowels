Hotel Buyer FAQ

Common questions from hotel operators.

What GSM towels do Australian hotels use?

Most Australian 3-star and 4-star hotels use 450–600 GSM bath towels — the sweet spot for plush feel, durability, and commercial laundry economics. Luxury 5-star properties typically step up to 600–700 GSM for a heavier drape. We supply all weights and can recommend based on your guest tier. See our full GSM guide for details.

Do you offer trade pricing for hotels?

Yes. We offer dedicated wholesale pricing for hotels and resorts, with trade accounts approved within 48 hours for ABN-verified Australian businesses. Apply for a trade account or request a tailored quote based on your room count.

How fast can you deliver hotel linen across Australia?

Standard dispatch is 2–5 business days from our Griffith NSW warehouse, with free shipping on orders over AU$100. Express options are available for urgent re-stocks — contact us directly to arrange.

Can I order samples before committing to a bulk order?

Absolutely. We strongly recommend sampling before any large order. Trade account holders can request samples of any 2–3 SKUs to evaluate in person before quoting on volume.

What linen do you supply to 5-star hotels?

Our luxury range includes 600–700 GSM combed ring-spun cotton bath towels, 200TC percale bed sheets, premium terry bathrobes, and microfibre pillows. For very high-end properties we can source on request — get in touch with your specifications.
