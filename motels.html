Motel Buyer FAQ

Common questions from motel operators.

What towels should a motel buy?

For Australian motels, 450–500 GSM cotton bath towels are the operator standard — balancing absorbency, durability, and laundry cost across high turnover. Avoid sub-300 GSM budget towels that fail within 40–80 washes. See our lifecycle cost analysis for the maths.

Do you supply linen to regional motels?

Yes — we ship Australia-wide from Griffith NSW. Many of our clients are regional NSW, VIC, QLD, and SA motels. Standard dispatch 2–5 business days, free shipping over AU$100.

Can I open a trade account as a small motel?

Absolutely. We work with motels of all sizes from 8-room family operations through to 60-room properties. Apply for a trade account — approved within 48 hours for ABN-verified businesses.

How often should motel linen be replaced?

For a busy motel, expect 7–8 months from a commercial-grade 450 GSM towel before noticeable wear, or 12–18 months between full-room linen refreshes. See our replacement frequency guide.

Do you do mixed-size orders?

Yes. We have no minimum order quantity — order exactly what your room mix needs. Different sheet sizes, towel quantities, and accessories all in one dispatch.
